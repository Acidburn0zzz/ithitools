About the ITHI Project

The ITHI project was started by ICANN in 2017, in the office of the CTO. The timeline of the project runs as follow:

  • 2017: definition of metrics, prototype tool chain.
  • Jan-Feb 2018: initial captures, M3, and M7.
    • We also captured M2, and single source M4 and M6.
  • March 2018: first data presented at ICANN meeting.
  • Apr-Jun 2018: pipeline automation, publish metrics on ICANN web site.
  • Continued updating and publication of metrics in the following years.

The project is managed by Alain Durand at ICANN and by Christian Huitema at Private Octopus Inc, under contract with ICANN. This initial web site is provided by Private Octopus Inc. As stated in the time line, these pages will soon be published through ICANN's web site.
